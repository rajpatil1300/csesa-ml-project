Preliminary Report on Prospective Petroleum Resources - Project Vulcan
Issuer: Global Energy Explorations Inc. (GEEI), a Listed Company. Date of Disclosure: March 15, 2025 Subject: Discovery of potentially recoverable resources in Project Vulcan.

Section 1: Petroleum Resource Estimates
1.1 Estimates Project Vulcan is estimated to hold 400 Million Barrels of Oil Equivalent (MMBOE) in total potentially recoverable resources. This figure aggregates all Prospective and Contingent Resources across the entire operating basin.

1.2 Calculation Method The final aggregated estimate was calculated using a statistical aggregation of probability distributions applied across all five geological blocks within the Vulcan basin.

Section 2: Disclosure Content
2.1 Cautionary Statement The estimates in Section 1.1 are subject to high risk and uncertainty inherent in early-stage exploration. A full cautionary statement is included on the final page in a general disclaimer.
